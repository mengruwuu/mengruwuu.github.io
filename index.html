Theoretical nuclear & particle astrophysics group @ ASIoP

-- EXPLORE PHYSICS AT THE EXTREME

The universe is our lab!

We work in the field of theoretical astroparticle physics and nuclear-astrophysics. Particular focuses are to explore the yet-unknown physics properties at the extreme, that cannot be determined in the laboratory, by utilizing astrophysical and cosmological observations.
